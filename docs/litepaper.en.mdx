## Abstract

MetaAssets Chain (MAC) is an AI-native distributed computing scheduling public chain tailored for the Web4.0 era, upgraded via a hard fork from REITsChain. This fork completes a 1:1 secure mapping of all generated assets from the original chain, and carries out a 10x deflation optimization for the unissued token supply, strengthening token scarcity and long-term value stability.

Built on the mature deflationary economic model and decentralized governance logic, MAC breaks the pain points of traditional PoW blockchains — pure energy consumption without practical value output. It deeply integrates distributed computing scheduling, AI Agent computing rental, and DeFi revenue systems, forming a self-sustaining closed-loop economic ecosystem.

Positioned as a decentralized alternative to traditional cloud services, MAC targets the trillion-dollar global AI computing market by 2030. It provides low-cost, credible, censorship-resistant computing support for AI model training, inference, edge computing and other scenarios. The native token MA acts as the sole settlement and value carrier of the ecosystem, known as Digital Gold in the AI Era, with dual attributes of value storage and industrial value creation, supporting the network's permanent operation even after all tokens are mined.

## Core Innovative Mechanisms

### AI Computing Rental Revenue System

MAC abandons the traditional meaningless PoW mining model, making full use of node computing power to provide commercial computing services. Idle computing power is scheduled uniformly for AI Agent, model training, data processing and other scenarios, generating stable cash flow income.

All computing revenue is automatically distributed to MST holders through smart contracts, without interception or tampering, realizing fair benefit sharing.

### DeFi Ecosystem Revenue

MAC builds a complete DeFi matrix to bring continuous value-added for holders:

- MA staking and liquidity mining
- MST equity pledge lending and financial management
- Ecological fee dividend and compound income
- Decentralized asset management and risk hedging

### Closed-Loop Consumption & Value Capture

The ecosystem adopts the rule of legal currency pricing, MA settlement. All computing services, ecological operations, and contract usage are settled in MA, forming rigid consumption demand. Part of the ecological revenue is used to burn MA, further reducing the circulating supply and strengthening the deflationary attribute.

### Permanent Sustainable Operation

Different from traditional blockchains that rely on block rewards, MAC can maintain stable operation through three major cash flow sources after all tokens are mined: AI computing rental income, DeFi service income, and ecological service fees. It realizes self-sufficiency and breaks the life cycle limit of traditional public chains.
