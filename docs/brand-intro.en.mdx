## Project Introduction

MetaAssets Chain (MAC) is upgraded from a hard fork of REITsChain, an AI-native distributed computing public chain built exclusively for Web4.0. The project completes a 1:1 secure mapping of all generated assets from the original chain, and implements a 10x deflation adjustment for the unissued supply, firmly establishing token scarcity and value stability at the foundational level.

MAC breaks the limitation of traditional public chains that only consume energy for mining, converting node computing power into AI Agent computing rental services, combined with DeFi ecological benefits to form a sustainable closed-loop economic system. Even after all MA tokens are fully issued, the network can still operate perpetually through computing services and on-chain revenue, building a decentralized "Distributed Amazon Cloud" infrastructure.

Backed by the trillion-dollar global AI computing market by 2030, MAC breaks the monopoly of centralized computing power, providing low-cost, highly trusted computing support for AI model training, inference computing, and edge computing. The MA token boasts dual attributes of value storage and real value creation, known as the Digital Gold in the AI Era. As the sole value and settlement carrier of the ecosystem, it reconstructs the global computing supply and value circulation system, becoming the core hub connecting the digital world and the AI physical industry.
