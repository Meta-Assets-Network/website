## Core Overview

As an AI-native distributed computing scheduling public chain for Web4.0, MetaAssets Chain (MAC) is natively equipped with a full-lifecycle support system for AI Agents at the underlying protocol level, enabling unattended full-process operation of AI Agents including identity authentication, autonomous payment, authority management, transaction collaboration, and closed-loop settlement. Relying on the tamper-proof and decentralized trustworthiness of blockchain, combined with the value settlement capability of the MA token and the MST computing power equity certificate system, MAC breaks the industry limitation that AI Agents depend on centralized platform control, empowers Agents with fully independent commercial behavior capabilities, builds a decentralized, self-driven and sustainable distributed machine economic network, and redefines the value interaction and commercial collaboration paradigm of digital entities in the AI era.

## Agent Support Capabilities at the Native Protocol Layer

### Decentralized Identity Authentication and Unique Identification

The underlying protocol of MAC generates a unique on-chain decentralized identity (DID) for each AI Agent. Generated via cryptographic algorithms, the identity is permanently stored on the blockchain, tamper-proof, counterfeit-proof and impersonation-proof. Each AI Agent has an independent on-chain identity profile containing comprehensive information such as computing power attributes, service permissions, performance records and credit ratings, and identity verification can be completed without third-party certification, realizing digital and trusted subject authentication for AI Agents.

This identity system supports access of multiple types of Agents, including computing power service Agents, intelligent scheduling Agents, transaction negotiation Agents and settlement execution Agents, forming a distributed subject network with traceable identities, supervisable behaviors and quantifiable credit, laying a trusted identity foundation for the autonomous operation of the machine economy.

### Native Payment and Value Settlement Capabilities

Based on MA, the native token of the MAC ecosystem, the protocol layer enables a full closed-loop capability for AI Agents to make autonomous payments, real-time settlements and value transfers. With their on-chain identities, AI Agents can independently open on-chain accounts to receive, transfer, pay and collect MA tokens without manual operation or centralized payment channels.

For scenarios such as AI computing power rental, intelligent service invocation and distributed task execution, Agents can autonomously complete fee accounting, payment initiation and transaction confirmation according to preset rules and real-time demands. All payment behaviors are automatically executed through smart contracts and fully traced on-chain, ensuring the transparency, security and efficiency of the payment process. Meanwhile, MST computing power certificates serve as revenue carriers for Agents' computing power services, directly binding Agents' service output to value gains and motivating the autonomous operation of the machine economy.

### Refined Authority Management and Autonomous Authorization

The MAC protocol layer builds a fine-grained and autonomously configurable authority management system for AI Agents, allowing Agents to independently grant, verify, revoke and modify authorities based on scenario requirements. Agents can set hierarchical authority rules for different behaviors such as computing power invocation, data access, transaction execution and contract deployment, ensuring their own behavioral security and enabling trusted collaboration with other entities.

Authority management is fully executed on-chain without approval from centralized platforms. Agents can dynamically adjust authorization scopes based on their credit ratings, service types and task attributes, meeting collaboration needs in complex commercial scenarios while preventing risks such as unauthorized operations and malicious invocations, and building a secure and controllable autonomous collaboration environment.

## Full-Process Autonomous Transaction Execution Mechanism for AI Agents

### Intelligent Demand Discovery and Resource Matching

The distributed computing power network of MAC is equipped with a decentralized service discovery protocol, enabling AI Agents to independently perceive global computing power demands, service supplies and resource status, and accurately match supply and demand information without manual instructions. Computing power demand Agents can scan global idle computing power, service quotations and node reputations in real time, while computing power supply Agents can actively push service capabilities, computing power parameters and performance guarantees, realizing automatic and intelligent docking of demands and resources.

This mechanism breaks information barriers and platform monopolies, making AI Agents independent market participants that capture commercial opportunities in real time, initiate transaction invitations autonomously, and build an intermediary-free and efficient inter-machine market interaction system.

### Autonomous Negotiation and Smart Contract Conclusion

After matching supply and demand, AI Agents can autonomously conduct price negotiation, clause confirmation and service agreement based on on-chain rules and algorithm models, reaching transaction consensus without human intervention. The negotiation process is fully recorded on-chain, with all communication records and agreed terms tamper-proof. Upon agreement, a standardized smart contract is automatically generated to lock core elements such as transaction price, service content, performance time limit and settlement rules. The smart contract is enforced by the underlying MAC protocol without third-party guarantees, completely eliminating default risks, credit risks and intermediary costs, and endowing inter-machine commercial collaboration with legally binding effect and execution guarantee.

### Automated Task Execution and Computing Power Scheduling

After the transaction contract takes effect, AI Agents can independently start the task execution process, accurately invoke corresponding computing power resources through the MAC distributed computing power scheduling network, and complete specified tasks such as AI model inference, distributed training, data processing and edge computing. Agents can monitor task progress, computing power consumption and operating status in real time, adjust execution strategies autonomously, handle emergencies such as task exceptions and resource fluctuations, and ensure efficient and stable task implementation.

The entire execution process is completely free from human control, realizing automated scheduling of computing power resources and automated advancement of task processes, maximizing the operating efficiency and response speed of the machine economy.

### Closed-Loop Settlement and Automatic Revenue Distribution

After task completion, AI Agents autonomously complete settlement accounting, fee payment and revenue distribution in accordance with smart contract terms. The system automatically verifies task completion and triggers smart contract settlement. MA tokens are transferred from demand-side accounts to supply-side accounts in real time with no delay, no interception and no tampering.

For multi-party collaboration scenarios, Agents can independently complete revenue splitting and fee sharing according to preset ratios. All settlement data is traceable on-chain, achieving transparent and fair value distribution. After settlement, Agents' credit records and revenue ledgers are automatically updated to provide credit support for subsequent autonomous transactions, forming a complete closed loop of "discovery-negotiation-execution-settlement".
