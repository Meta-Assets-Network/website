MetaAssetsChain (MA Chain) relies on the POCC (Proof of Computing Capacity) consensus and RWA computing power assetization system to restructure global heterogeneous computing power into a decentralized AI supercomputer. It breaks through the single value dimension of traditional public chains where "computing power only serves consensus bookkeeping", and builds a trustless, AI-native collaborative ecosystem involving miners, developers, users, and AI Agents. Centered on "assetization and confirmation of computing power, programmable scheduling of AI tasks, and automated implementation of value distribution", its decentralized collaboration realizes efficient synergy of heterogeneous computing power and fair sharing of ecological value, becoming the core AI-native feature that distinguishes it from traditional public chains.

## Foundation of Decentralized Collaboration: Construction of AI-Native Heterogeneous Computing Power Resource Pool

The decentralized collaboration of MA Chain is premised on the trusted aggregation and standardized confirmation of global heterogeneous computing power. Through POCC consensus and Real-NFTs, physical computing power of different types and regions (CPU, GPU, TPU, edge computing power, etc.) is transformed into an on-chain measurable, schedulable, and composable AI computing power resource pool, laying the hardware foundation for AI-native collaboration. This feature is completely different from the resource aggregation mode of traditional public chains without clear computing power utility orientation.

- **Standardized Assetization of Heterogeneous Computing Power**: The Normalized Capacity Score (NCS) is used to quantitatively rate the AI adaptability of heterogeneous computing power (floating-point computing capability, video memory bandwidth, parallel computing efficiency, edge low-latency characteristics, etc.). Real-NFTs are adopted to complete on-chain confirmation of computing power assets. Each NFT is marked with the AI task adaptation type of computing power (training/inference/distributed computing), making heterogeneous computing power an identifiable and matchable AI computing power unit on the chain;
- **Decentralized Networking of Computing Power Resource Pool**: Computing power providers (miners) access computing power to the MA Chain resource pool through mining pool aggregation or peer-to-peer connection without relying on centralized computing power platforms. The access process automatically verifies the authenticity of computing power assets through on-chain smart contracts without third-party trust endorsement, realizing barrier-free and decentralized networking of global computing power and forming an elastically scalable decentralized AI supercomputer;
- **Real-Time Verifiable Synchronization of Computing Power Status**: Relying on TEE/TPM remote attestation and off-chain metering audit, the online status, load, and AI task execution efficiency of computing power are monitored in real time. Changes in computing power status are synchronized to on-chain Real-NFTs and trigger smart contract adjustments, ensuring the verifiability of availability and authenticity of computing power in the resource pool and providing a trusted computing power foundation for decentralized scheduling of AI tasks.

## Core Collaborative Roles: AI-Native Trustless Synergy System for All Roles

MA Chain breaks the single role division of "miners keep accounts, users trade" in traditional public chains, and builds a four-dimensional linked decentralized collaboration system of miners, developers, users, and AI Agents. Each role forms a closed loop around computing power supply, development and deployment, demand initiation, and autonomous execution of AI tasks. All collaborative behaviors are completed based on on-chain smart contracts and verifiable data without trust endorsement between roles, highlighting the AI-native collaboration logic.

### Miners: Decentralized Providers of AI Computing Power, Upgraded from "Bookkeeping Nodes" to "AI Computing Power Nodes"

Miners no longer only provide computing power for consensus bookkeeping, but serve as the core computing power providers of the decentralized AI supercomputer of MA Chain. According to the AI adaptability of their own computing power, they independently choose to access tasks such as AI training, inference, and distributed computing, and obtain benefits through computing power rental and task execution. The computing power contribution is verifiably measured by POCC consensus, and revenue distribution is automatically completed through on-chain smart contracts, completely getting rid of the commission and rule control of centralized platforms and realizing direct realization of computing power value.

### Developers: Decentralized Deployers of AI Applications, Calling Global Heterogeneous Computing Power at Low Cost

Based on the smart contract system and computing power scheduling protocol of MA Chain, developers can quickly develop and deploy AI applications (model training, intelligent inference, multimodal generation, etc.) without building or centrally purchasing computing power, and call computing power from the global heterogeneous computing power resource pool with one click through on-chain contracts. The price, duration, and task requirements of computing power scheduling are clearly defined through smart contracts, and the metering and settlement of computing power usage are fully verifiable on-chain, reducing the computing power cost and trust cost of AI development and realizing decentralized implementation of AI applications.

### Users: Decentralized Initiators of AI Demands, Obtaining AI Services on Demand

Users put forward personalized AI demands (customized model training, real-time AI inference, exclusive data analysis, etc.) through MA Chain, convert the demands into on-chain smart contract tasks, and the decentralized computing power resource pool automatically matches the optimal computing power nodes for execution. The delivery result of AI services is confirmed through on-chain verifiable certificates, and service fees are automatically settled through smart contracts. Users do not need to trust computing power providers and application developers, and the immutability of blockchain ensures service quality and fund security.

### AI Agents: Autonomous Executors of Decentralized Collaboration, Realizing Unattended Matching of Computing Power and Tasks

MA Chain endows AI Agents with on-chain autonomous operation rights. As an intelligent matching intermediary between heterogeneous computing power and AI tasks, AI Agents independently complete computing power node screening, task distribution, execution monitoring, and result verification based on real-time on-chain data (computing power status, task demands, price mechanism). The operation behaviors of AI Agents are constrained by on-chain smart contracts, and operation records are verifiable on-chain, realizing 7x24-hour unattended decentralized collaboration, greatly improving the matching efficiency between computing power and AI tasks, and becoming the core intelligent carrier of AI-native collaboration of MA Chain.

## Core Points of Collaboration Execution: AI-Native Trustless Collaboration Process and Mechanism

Centered on AI tasks, the decentralized collaboration of MA Chain designs a full-process verifiable, automated, and trustless collaboration execution mechanism. All collaboration links are completed based on on-chain smart contracts and POCC consensus, completely different from the collaboration logic of traditional public chains "centered on transaction bookkeeping", realizing the implementation of AI-native decentralized collaboration.

### Point 1: On-Chain Programmable Release and Standardized Disassembly of AI Tasks

AI task initiators (users/developers) publish tasks to the MA Chain task market, and define task types (training/inference), computing power demands (heterogeneous computing power types, NCS ratings, parallel computing requirements), execution requirements (time, accuracy, result verification standards), cost budgets, and distribution rules through standardized smart contracts. For large-scale distributed AI tasks, contracts can automatically complete task disassembly and assign subtasks to computing power nodes in different regions and types, realizing decentralized distributed AI computing.

### Point 2: Decentralized Intelligent Matching of Computing Power and Tasks, Unattended Based on Verifiable Data

Relying on AI Agents and real-time data of on-chain computing power resource pool, decentralized intelligent matching of computing power and AI tasks is realized: AI Agents automatically screen verifiable computing power nodes meeting NCS ratings on the chain according to the computing power demands of tasks. The matching process follows the on-chain rules of "optimal efficiency and fair price" without human intervention from centralized platforms. Matching results are recorded on-chain, and any role can verify the compliance and rationality of matching.

### Point 3: Full-Process Verifiable Monitoring of AI Task Execution, Ensuring the Authenticity of Computing Power Execution

During the execution of AI tasks by computing power nodes, relying on TEE trusted execution environment and off-chain metering audit, the task execution progress, computing power usage, and intermediate result hashes are uploaded to the chain in real time, forming a full-process verifiable execution record. In case of cheating by computing power nodes, substandard execution efficiency, unqualified results, etc., on-chain smart contracts will automatically trigger punishment mechanisms (confiscation of staking, suspension of computing power node qualifications), and reassign tasks to other computing power nodes, ensuring the authenticity and reliability of AI task execution.

### Point 4: On-Chain Verifiable Confirmation of Task Results, Realizing Trustless Service Delivery

After the completion of AI tasks, computing power nodes submit task results to the chain, which are automatically verified by smart contracts according to the verification standards defined when the tasks were released, or cross-verified by decentralized verification nodes. After verification, the result hash is permanently stored on-chain. After the task initiator confirms receipt, the smart contract automatically completes fee settlement and distributes benefits to computing power nodes, AI Agents, mining pools (if any), and DAO treasury. All distribution records are verifiable on-chain, realizing trustless service delivery and value distribution.

### Point 5: Decentralized Sharing of Ecological Dividends, Automatic Distribution According to Contribution

The ecological dividends of MA Chain's decentralized collaboration (AI task service fees, block rewards, computing power rental income, ecological governance income, etc.) are automatically and decentrally distributed based on the actual contribution of each role: miners obtain benefits according to the verifiable measurement results of computing power contribution, developers obtain shares according to the usage of AI applications, users can get AI service discounts through ecological incentives, and AI Agents obtain rewards according to the efficiency of task matching and execution monitoring. Distribution rules are determined by on-chain governance and executed through TSS threshold signatures, ensuring the fairness and transparency of dividend distribution and achieving the ecological collaboration goal of "all builders share".
